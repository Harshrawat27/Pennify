Spendler — Personal Finance & Budget Tracker

Take control of your money with Spendler. Track every rupee, dollar, or euro you spend, set budgets that actually work, and build savings goals — all in one clean, fast app.

TRACK YOUR SPENDING
Log income and expenses in seconds. Attach receipts by scanning them with your camera. Organize transactions across multiple accounts — cash, bank, credit card, or digital wallet. Bookmark important transactions to find them later.

BUDGETS THAT MAKE SENSE
Set a monthly spending limit and category-level budgets. See exactly how much you've spent versus your budget in real time. Get a clear picture of where your money goes every month.

SAVINGS GOALS
Create goals for anything — emergency fund, travel, home, tech. Track how much you've saved and how far you have to go. Add contributions and mark goals as complete when you get there.

RECURRING PAYMENTS
Never lose track of subscriptions and bills. Add your recurring payments with billing dates, amounts, and frequencies. Get notified the day before anything renews.

PEOPLE & SHARED EXPENSES
Track money you owe or are owed. Record payments with dates and notes and see the running balance for each person.

SMART RULES
Set keyword-based rules to automatically categorize transactions as you log them. Save time on repetitive categorization.

REPORTS & INSIGHTS
View your spending trends by week, month, or year. See breakdowns by category and drill into sub-categories. Compare months and spot patterns in your financial habits.

ASK PENNY — AI FINANCE ASSISTANT
Chat with Penny, your built-in AI financial assistant. Ask questions about your spending, get insights about your habits, and understand your finances better.

MORE FEATURES
• 25+ currencies with proper locale formatting
• Offline support — log transactions without internet, syncs automatically when back online
• Daily reminders and weekly spending reports
• Hide balance for privacy in public
• Sign in with Google or Apple — your data is always backed up
